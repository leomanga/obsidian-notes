# Proposizione 4
>Sia $V$ [[Spazio euclideo]], $\dim(V)=n$
>$A,B$ due [[sottospazi lineari]] di $V$.
>Allora$$
\begin{split}
(i)\qquad &{(A\cap B)^{\perp}=A^\perp+B^{\perp}}\\
(ii)\qquad &{A+B}=(A^{\perp}\cap B^{\perp})^{\perp}\\
(iii)\qquad&(A+B)^{\perp}=A^{\perp}\cap B^{\perp}\end{split}$$
